Journey of how I started this small business

June 2019

I had just gotten out of the military and no purpose in life. I was lost and trying to figure out what to do.

June 2021

I had stumbled upon pottery, but since I was focusing in pottery on free time outside of work my boss decided to let me go from work because I was "not all in for his company" he did not like I had a hobby

June 2023

I started going all in this company. Thank to you guys this small business has been taking off. I enjoy working with my hands everyday and creating something new from nothing.

Present

Demand has sky rocketed. Thanks to that I have my wife helping me with the business. Also I created this website This way I can keep in touch with everyone.
