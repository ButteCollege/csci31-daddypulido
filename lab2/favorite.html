The Beauty of Blues and Teals

Colors have a way of setting the mood, and the shades of blue-green I’ve chosen for this design bring a fresh, modern, and calming vibe. Whether it’s the soft background or the deeper accent shades, every color plays a role in making the page feel balanced and inviting.

A Breath of Fresh Air

The light aqua background (#c6e4e4) is like a soft breeze—it keeps things airy and easy on the eyes. It’s the perfect foundation for a design that feels open and refreshing, almost like a coastal morning.

The Perfect Contrast

To add structure and contrast, I used deep teals (#065f5f and #083f3f) for elements like buttons and divs. These shades are rich, strong, and give the design a bit of depth without being too harsh. They stand out just enough while still blending harmoniously with the background.

A Splash of Personality

The muted teal rgb(85, 170, 170) brings a little extra character. It’s noticeable, but not overwhelming—just enough to grab attention. And with hover effects that soften the colors, everything feels interactive and dynamic.
